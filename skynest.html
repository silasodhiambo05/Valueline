Property Description

Experience the epitome of sophistication stepping into Skynest Residence, Nairobi’s incredible delight, meticulously curated to cater for your needs. It is situated along Mkungu Close, Westlands and is highly sought after due to its close proximity to shopping malls, 5-star hotels and restaurants within a 1 km radius, Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, Nairobi SGR station, Central Business District & Westlands entertainment district. Skynest boasts furnished and unfurnished apartments with 1-, 2-, 3- and 4-bedrooms that offers a panoramic city view. It is crowned with a trendy rooftop lounge with amenities ranging from resident lawn and party area, barbeque and buffet area with a capacity for 400 guests a heated infinity swimming pool, state of the art gym and sauna, a squash court and a games room with snooker and table tennis.
