WHEN LIFE GIVES YOU RAIN: WEAR BOOTS AND GO HOUSE HUNTING

The rainy season is here with us, and if you want to be a successful real estate investor, keep reading to learn why rainy days are great for viewing homes.

Let’s face it home-buying is a competitive process with a lot of Real Estate players who use all the tricks and tools of trade to ensure they stay afloat. If you are like most people, you ideally wouldn’t go outside to house-hunt or view a property in the chilly, wet weather; hence this makes this a great time to identify the flaws that most properties would present due to constant wear and tear.

Counter-intuitive right? From a real estate point of view you get to view properties without any rush, get in-depth information on not-yet listed properties.

Rain will help both the buyer and their agent when they are out looking at homes and considering making an offer. Also helps your home inspector look really competent

Reasons why rain is your friend when purchasing a property

1. Internal damage assessments are more effective as internal water damage that would not be visible on a sunny day are easily identifiable on a rainy, wet day where chances of seeing leaks, stains or smells occur
2. Rain will help you get your mind in the gutter and identify water related red flags such as drainage, roofing and standing pools of water
3. Quality of the housing material is often affected by harsh weather conditions like paintings on doors, window paintings, electric pillars and so on.
4. House condition, neighboring community and extra features about a property is information that a potential home-owner may seek from the real estate agent, neighbors and governing bodies
