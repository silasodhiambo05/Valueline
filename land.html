Land Sales

  1. Home
  2. Pages
  3. Land Sales

Determining an appropriate asking price is amongst the most difficult aspects of representing land. Sales comparables are far fewer compared to other types of commercial real estate therefore getting an “apples to apples” comparison is very difficult.

Our land sales experts consider all of the following when recommending pricing:

1.Zoning
2.Location of on and off-site improvements
3.Topography
4.Potential users
5.Desired timing

There is a lot of land available in Kenya and so in order to properly market your property, we need to provide enough up front information to developers, users, speculators, and their agents to get and keep them interested in your property. Our high-quality land brochures include:

1.Aerials and maps
2.Zoning information
3.Potential site plans
4.Traffic counts
5.Demographics and business generators
6.Projected marketplace growth

You want to hear about buyer activity for your property. You want to know what Value Line Consulting is doing to market your property. You want to know what is happening in the market. You want us to communicate with you. We understand and we endeavor to keep in touch either with a phone call, a quick text, or an email.
