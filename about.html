About Us

Who are we? Well, we are Value Line Consulting Limited, a top-tier professional real estate consulting company with a rich heritage and experience in delivering real estate solutions to a diverse clientele across Africa.
Like many companies, it began as a side business more than 10 years ago while working for other real estate companies. We have since grown into one of the most trusted real estate company in Kenya with a wide portfolio that features reputable brands.
Our business is underpinned and constantly fueled by our passion to see our clients happy. To that end, we have perfectly crafted the art of listening and understanding what you need and want.
Working with a highly qualified team, we offer expert Valuation and Advisory, Property Management, Estate Agency, Real Estate Research and Consulting and Property Development Consulting Services.

Our Story

Our story and exceptional heritage in delivering excellent real estate solutions started way back in 2012. Value Line Consulting began its journey out of a quest to bridge the gap in the levels of professionalism within the real estate industry.
Back during the old days as a nascent industry, professionalism was quite a challenge as practitioners tried to learn the ropes. As a result, clients along the entire real estate value chain had inconsistent customer service. However, there were limited options. Companies that were up to par, charged a premium, way beyond the reach of the masses. Something had to be done, fast.
Coincidentally, two people were observing the events playing out from within whilst in different parts of the vast industry. They felt a strong urge to change the status quo. Like soul mates whose hearts and minds connected telepathically, the universe brought the two founders together to form Value Line Consulting Limited.
United by the desire and inspiration to upscale professional services in the industry, they embarked on a mission to offer real estate clients more value. The company’s founders recognized and appreciated the fact that as a property investor, homeowner, or tenant, it takes great effort and sacrifice in finding the perfect property solutions that suit your unique tastes as well as preferences.
Since our founding over 10 years ago, Value Line Consulting has continued to meet clients’ property solution requirements. We have kept pace with emerging trends, approaches, and methodologies to ensure that we serve you remarkably well throughout all the real estate value chains.
Today, driven by our unrelenting passion for your success, we have perfectly crafted the art of listening and understanding what you need and want. We plunge deep into your dream and come up with a tailored solution for you. Whether it is about getting you the best rental yields from your property investment, advising on site selection for development, taking care of your property, or assessing the market value of your property to allow you to get a fair value from its sale, we can do it all. It is not because we are super a company. Rather, it is because we would like you to be guaranteed superior quality services every single time you go out shopping for real estate solutions.
