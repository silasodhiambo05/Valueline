Property Description

M&T ONE has launched a modern warehouse facility with offices andablution facilities in the heart of Tatu City Special Economic Zone (SEZ).The state-of-the-art development is expected to plug in the ongoing shortage of warehouses in the country.

The demand for grade-A warehousing coupled with magnificient office spacefacilities delivers significant cost savings and efficiency for users, currently exceedsthe supply within the main capital city Nairobi and far across other regions in thecountry thus getting suitable warehousing facilities has become a challenge.Tatu City offers haven of business support for it has enhanced infrastructurefacilities such as roads, Power, Water, Waste Management and ICT whichhas made the City to become an ideal operating environment for businessesentering or expanding within the region. It has been Declared Special Economiczone (SEZ).

Due to the traffic and inadequate space for the business expansion in NairobiCBD, M&T ONE has now ventured its logistics services in the newly region whichnow has seen many developments taking great heights.
